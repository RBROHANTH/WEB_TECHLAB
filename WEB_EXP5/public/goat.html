About Our Goats

The native goat breeds of Tamil Nadu, such as Kanni Adu and Salem Black, are known for their resilience and adaptability. These goats are well-suited for meat and milk production, thriving in diverse climatic conditions. With a compact yet strong body structure, they are naturally disease-resistant and require minimal maintenance.

Our goats are raised in a free-range environment, allowing them to graze naturally and maintain optimal health. They are provided with a balanced diet of organic fodder, ensuring high-quality milk and meat production.

Our Farming Practices

At RBR Farms, we follow sustainable and ethical goat farming practices. Our goats graze on natural pastures, promoting their well-being while ensuring an eco-friendly approach to livestock farming. We avoid the use of synthetic hormones or antibiotics, focusing instead on organic nutrition and traditional care.

We also preserve age-old farming techniques such as rotational grazing, which allows pastures to regenerate naturally and provides the goats with fresh, nutrient-rich forage. Our commitment to sustainability ensures that our farming methods are both environmentally friendly and beneficial to the animals.

Why Choose Us?

RBR Farms takes pride in raising high-quality, naturally bred goats in a healthy and sustainable environment. Our animals are nurtured with great care, ensuring they grow strong and produce superior-quality milk and meat.

By choosing RBR Farms, you support ethical goat farming and help preserve Tamil Nadu’s native breeds. Whether you’re looking for fresh organic goat milk, premium-quality meat, or simply want to learn about sustainable farming, we are your trusted source for authenticity and excellence.
