Kangayam Cattle

The Kangayam breed, native to Tamil Nadu, is known for its strength and agility. These medium-sized cattle are ideal for farm work, with strong legs and a well-developed hump. Their compact bodies, small erect ears, and dark expressive eyes make them distinct.

With their remarkable endurance, Kangayam bulls are used in traditional bull races like Rekla and Jallikattu. Additionally, these cattle were historically used for lifting water in Yetram, an ancient irrigation system.

Dairy & Farm Benefits

While Kangayam cows produce a moderate amount of milk, their milk is rich in nutrients. The breed is highly valued for its resilience, requiring minimal maintenance and thriving in local climatic conditions.

At RBR Farms, we take pride in maintaining purebred Kangayam cattle, ensuring they receive proper care and sustainable grazing areas.
